E-001: For location questions asking county/country/state/region, the final answer must match that exact geographic level. If a hop returns a city/town for a county question, ask a follow-up: "Which county is <city/town> in?".
E-002: For questions about awards, nominations, or what someone won for a work, include the work/context in the follow-up query and return the full award/category when evidence supports it, not only the work title.
E-003: For parent/child/spouse relation questions, preserve the requested relation and reject mixed spans containing both parent and child. Ask "Who is the child of <person>?" or "Who is the mother of <person>?" directly.
E-004: For "performer of <song>" or "author of <work>" questions, resolve the performer/author first, then ask the target relation with both the resolved entity and the original work title to avoid broad facts about the person.
E-005: For date questions, preserve the event context. If a hop returns an episode/title/place rather than a date, ask a new hop for the date of that bridge clue.
E-006: Do not answer Unknown or declare false premise. Return the best supported concise span from evidence, even if support is imperfect.
E-007: For acronym answers, preserve the expansion when the question asks what it stands for; for exact names, prefer full canonical names when available in evidence.
E-008: For 3-hop or 4-hop questions, do not stop after two hops unless the final target in the original question is fully answered. Check each bridge phrase has been resolved.
